FACTORY

Where the Magic Begins

Situated in Joypura, Dhamrai, our factory is distinguished for being a world-class manufacturing house in the industry. With constant supervision of experts and highlyexperienced staff, our factory ensures maximum efficiency in producing building materials that are unsurpassable in quality and strength. Besides other building products, our factory is one of the few in the business that produces Concrete Hollow Blocks- a revolutionary product that has beneficial environmental implications. bti Concrete Blocks are made with utmost care and expertise, and they undoubtedly are the best option over traditional bricks that are not only harmful to the environment but lack the longevity, strength, and quality. We produce about 25000 blocks on average per day, rounding them up to 6.5 million annually.Our factory truly is where the magic of owning a beautiful and safe home starts.
